Parking and Transportation

Tips and resources on traveling to the venue from your home

Metro and Shuttle Service

Please exit the metro at Silk Institute, a shuttle service leaves to the venue location every hour, looks for signs on the wind shield and rear glass of Tempo Travelers for Nesara Center for Culture- Ankita weds Anil.

Event Parking

Parking is available for upto 300 cars on a first come first serve basis. Please follow the signs to the parking area at the venue. Here are a few images to give you a heads up of the location.

Transportation Form

Please fill the form below if you need transportation to attend the event.

A Pick up point will be assigned in you locality from which transportation is arranged. We will personally give you a call providing you a time and information of the diver and vehicle (license, make and description) for you to board on time. The driver will not wait for more than 15 mins at your pick up point (unless communicated in case of delays).
